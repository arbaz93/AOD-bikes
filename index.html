A new generation of vintage bike

We have been a pioneer in the development and production of bikes, catering to the needs of cyclists of all ages and skill levels. Since its inception, our company has been committed to providing high-quality bicycles that are built to last. Our bikes are known for their durability, comfort, and performance, and have become a popular choice among cyclists across the country.

Vintage Oliva

Our company was founded in 1926 by a group of passionate cyclists who aimed to create high-quality bicycles for enthusiasts. The company was initially named after its founders and was known for its hand-crafted bicycles, which were highly regarded for their durability and performance.

La Boriosa

The company's team of skilled engineers and designers work tirelessly to create bicycles that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing. They use only the best materials and the latest technology to ensure that each bicycle is of the highest quality.
